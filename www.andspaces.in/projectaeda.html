An interactive office space designed including work area as well as breakout spaces. The idea behind the design includes fresh colors, spacious interiors and an informal work-space.

Project Highlights

  • Cozy Pantry Area

    The pantry area is designed with fresh colors and window seatings so that it can serve as an alternative breakout work-space as well.

  • Breakout Work Space

    There is a separate seating provided in the work space for small group meetings or breaks.
